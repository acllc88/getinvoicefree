GST Information for India

GST (Goods and Services Tax) in India has multiple rates: 0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%. Most services are taxed at 18%. Businesses with turnover above ₹20 lakh (₹10 lakh in special category states) must register for GST and include their GSTIN on invoices.

What to Include on a India Invoice

  • Your business name, address, and contact details
  • GSTIN (GST Identification Number)
  • PAN Number
  • Client name and billing address
  • Unique invoice number and issue date
  • Due date and payment terms
  • Itemised list of goods or services
  • GST rate and amount (if registered)
  • Total amount due in INR ₹
  • Your payment details

Payment Methods in India

NEFT, RTGS, IMPS, and UPI are the most common payment methods in India. Include your bank account details or UPI ID in the notes field.

Payment Terms in India

Payment terms vary widely in India. Net 30 to Net 45 is common for B2B. For freelancers and consultants, 50% advance is often requested.
