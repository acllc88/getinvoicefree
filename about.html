About Free Invoice Generator

We built Free Invoice Generator because we were tired of invoice tools that promised "free" and then demanded a signup, a credit card, or charged you to download your own work.

Our mission is simple: give every freelancer, contractor, and small business owner a professional invoice tool that is genuinely free, forever — no account, no watermark, no tricks.

Who we built this for

Freelancers who invoice 1–5 clients a month and don't need a $30/month accounting subscription. Contractors who need a clean PDF to send alongside a quote. Small business owners who want to look professional without the complexity.

How we keep it free

We display non-intrusive Google AdSense advertisements below the tool. These ads are clearly labelled and never placed inside the invoice generator itself. Your invoice remains ad-free and watermark-free.

Your data stays with you

Everything you type stays in your browser. We never see your invoice data. It never touches our servers because we don't have any — this is a static website hosted on Netlify.
