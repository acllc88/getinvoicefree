Can I use the Word template in Google Docs?

Yes. Upload the .docx file to Google Drive, right-click and choose "Open with Google Docs". All formatting is preserved. You can then edit and download as PDF or Word.

Does the Excel template work in Google Sheets?

Yes. Upload the .xlsx file to Google Drive and open with Google Sheets. The automatic total formulas work in Google Sheets. Note that some formatting may differ slightly.

How do I add my logo to the Word template?

Open the .docx file in Word or Google Docs. Click on the business name area in the header and insert your logo image using Insert > Picture. Resize as needed. The online generator has a built-in logo upload feature that's much faster.
