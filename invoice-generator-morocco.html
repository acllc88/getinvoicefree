TVA Information for Morocco

TVA (Taxe sur la Valeur Ajoutée) in Morocco has a standard rate of 20% for most goods and services. Reduced rates apply to certain goods (7%, 10%, 14%). Businesses must include their ICE number (Identifiant Commun de l'Entreprise) on all invoices.

What to Include on a Morocco Invoice

  • Your business name, address, and contact details
  • ICE Number (Identifiant Commun de l'Entreprise)
  • RC Number (Registre du Commerce)
  • Client name and billing address
  • Unique invoice number and issue date
  • Due date and payment terms
  • Itemised list of goods or services
  • TVA rate and amount (if registered)
  • Total amount due in MAD
  • Your payment details

Payment Methods in Morocco

Virement bancaire (bank transfer) is the standard payment method for B2B in Morocco. Include your RIB (Relevé d'Identité Bancaire) in the notes field.

Payment Terms in Morocco

Net 30 to Net 60 is common in Morocco. The law limits payment terms to 60 days for commercial transactions between businesses.
